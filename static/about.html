About TravelSight

TravelSight is website made to reduce the stress and uncertainty that comes with travelling. All you need to do to get started is input your destination, how long you will be there for, and which data interests you. Once you do that, you will be directed to another page with all the information you are interested in regarding your destination. That's it! Feel free to try it out by clicking on the 'Home' button and following the steps mentioned above!
